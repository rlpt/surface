## Parties

**(1) RICHARD TARGETT** of [HOME ADDRESS] (the **"Assignor"**)

**(2) FORMABI LTD**, a company registered in England and Wales with company number [COMPANY NUMBER], whose registered office is at [REGISTERED OFFICE ADDRESS] (the **"Company"**)

## Background

(A) The Assignor is the sole director and sole shareholder of the Company.

(B) Prior to and since the incorporation of the Company, the Assignor has created certain intellectual property relating to the business of the Company.

(C) The Assignor wishes to assign to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to that intellectual property, so that the Company is the sole legal and beneficial owner of all intellectual property used in or relating to its business.

## Definitions

In this Deed:

**"Assigned IP"** means all Intellectual Property Rights in and to the Assigned Works.

**"Assigned Works"** means:

(a) all software, source code, object code, algorithms, data structures, databases, APIs, scripts, configuration files, and related documentation created by the Assignor at any time prior to or since the incorporation of the Company, to the extent that such works relate to the business of the Company, including but not limited to:

- the Formabi form builder application (Elm frontend, Rust backend, and all associated components)
- the company-as-code system (Nix configurations, shell scripts, flake definitions, module system)
- all infrastructure code, deployment scripts, and server configurations
- all development tooling, build systems, and CI/CD configurations

(b) all designs, wireframes, mockups, user interface designs, user experience flows, graphics, logos, brand assets, and visual materials created by the Assignor relating to the business of the Company;

(c) all business plans, financial models, market research, pitch decks, presentations, and strategic documents created by the Assignor relating to the business of the Company;

(d) all domain names registered by or on behalf of the Assignor for the purposes of the Company's business, including but not limited to:

- [LIST DOMAIN NAMES — e.g. formabi.com, formabi.app, zolanic.space, etc.]

(e) all trade marks (whether registered or unregistered), trade names, brand names, logos, and goodwill associated with the name "Formabi" and any other marks used by the Assignor in connection with the Company's business;

(f) all inventions, discoveries, improvements, know-how, trade secrets, and confidential information created or acquired by the Assignor relating to the business of the Company;

(g) all other works of authorship, materials, and creations of any kind made by the Assignor relating to the business of the Company;

in each case whether created before or after the date of incorporation of the Company, and whether in finished or unfinished form.

**"Intellectual Property Rights"** means all intellectual property rights of any kind, including:

(a) copyright, database rights, design rights (registered and unregistered), and moral rights;

(b) patents, patent applications, and rights to apply for patents;

(c) trade marks (registered and unregistered), service marks, and rights in trade names, domain names, and goodwill;

(d) rights in know-how, trade secrets, and confidential information;

(e) all rights of a similar nature arising or subsisting anywhere in the world;

(f) all renewals, extensions, continuations, and reversions of the foregoing.

## Assignment

### 1. Assignment of Intellectual Property Rights

(1) With effect from the date of this Deed, the Assignor hereby assigns to the Company, with full title guarantee, all right, title, and interest (legal and beneficial, present and future) in and to the Assigned IP, to hold absolutely for the full term of such rights (including any renewals, revivals, reversions, and extensions) throughout the world.

(2) The assignment under clause 1(1) includes the right to sue for and recover damages, profits, and other remedies in respect of any infringement of the Assigned IP occurring before, on, or after the date of this Deed.

### 2. Waiver of moral rights

(1) To the extent permitted by law, the Assignor irrevocably and unconditionally waives all moral rights in the Assigned Works to which the Assignor is now or may at any future time be entitled under Chapter IV of Part I of the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 and under any similar legislation in force from time to time anywhere in the world.

(2) The waiver in clause 2(1) extends to the benefit of the Company, its licensees, successors, and assigns.

### 3. Consideration

The assignment under this Deed is made in consideration of:

(a) the sum of £1.00 (one pound sterling), receipt of which the Assignor hereby acknowledges; and

(b) the Ordinary Shares in the Company already held by the Assignor.

### 4. Further assurance

(1) The Assignor shall at the request and cost of the Company:

(a) execute all documents and do all acts and things as may be necessary to vest the Assigned IP in the Company and to enable the Company to enjoy the full benefit of the Assigned IP;

(b) assist the Company in applying for registration of any registrable Intellectual Property Rights included in the Assigned IP;

(c) assist the Company in any proceedings relating to the Assigned IP, including providing witness statements or giving evidence.

(2) The obligations in clause 4(1) shall survive the termination of any employment or engagement of the Assignor by the Company.

### 5. Warranties

The Assignor warrants and represents to the Company that:

(a) the Assignor is the sole legal and beneficial owner of the Assigned IP and has full right, power, and authority to assign it;

(b) the Assigned IP is free from all encumbrances, liens, charges, licences, and third-party rights, and no third party has been granted any rights in respect of the Assigned IP;

(c) so far as the Assignor is aware, the Assigned Works are original and do not infringe the Intellectual Property Rights of any third party;

(d) the Assignor has not done and will not do anything to prejudice or diminish the Assigned IP;

(e) no claim has been made, and so far as the Assignor is aware no claim is threatened, by any third party in respect of the Assigned IP;

(f) the Assignor has disclosed to the Company all material information relating to the Assigned IP of which the Assignor is aware;

(g) where any of the Assigned Works incorporate open source software, the Assignor has complied with the terms of the applicable open source licences and has disclosed to the Company a complete list of all open source software used in or bundled with the Assigned Works.

### 6. Third-party materials

(1) The Assignor acknowledges that certain Assigned Works may incorporate:

(a) open source software components used under their respective licences; and

(b) third-party libraries, frameworks, or tools used under licence.

(2) Nothing in this Deed purports to assign any Intellectual Property Rights owned by third parties. The assignment relates only to the Assignor's own original contributions and Intellectual Property Rights.

(3) The Assignor shall provide to the Company a schedule of all third-party materials and open source components incorporated in the Assigned Works, together with the applicable licence terms, within 14 days of the date of this Deed.

### 7. Power of attorney

The Assignor irrevocably appoints the Company (acting through any of its directors) as the Assignor's attorney to execute any document and do any act in the name of the Assignor which is necessary or desirable to give effect to this Deed, to the extent that the Assignor has failed to do so within 10 Business Days of a written request from the Company.

### 8. No implied licence

The Assignor acknowledges that from the date of this Deed, the Assignor has no right, title, or interest in the Assigned IP and no licence (express or implied) to use the Assigned IP, except as may be granted by the Company in writing (including under any service agreement between the Assignor and the Company).

### 9. Entire agreement

(1) This Deed constitutes the entire agreement between the parties relating to its subject matter and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and arrangements (whether written or oral) relating to the assignment of Intellectual Property Rights from the Assignor to the Company.

(2) Nothing in this clause limits or excludes any liability for fraud.

### 10. Governing law and jurisdiction

(1) This Deed is governed by and shall be construed in accordance with the laws of England and Wales.

(2) The courts of England and Wales shall have exclusive jurisdiction to settle any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Deed.
