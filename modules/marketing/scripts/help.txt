Marketing (decks, handbook, legal docs, diagrams & docs):
  marketing serve             Live preview decks with hot reload
  marketing build             Build all decks to out/marketing/
  marketing handbook-build    Build handbook to out/marketing/handbook/
  marketing handbook-serve    Build + serve handbook at :8000
  marketing legal-build       Build legal docs to out/marketing/legal/
  marketing legal-serve       Build + serve legal handbook at :8001
  marketing legal-pdf <file>  Build a legal doc to PDF (or --all)
  marketing diagrams          Build .mmd diagrams to out/marketing/diagrams/
  marketing push-doc <file>   Push markdown to Google Docs (formatted)
  marketing push-doc-setup    One-time Google Drive OAuth setup
