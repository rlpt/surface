**DATE:** _[date]_

## Parties

1. **The Company:** Formabi Ltd, a company incorporated in England and Wales under company number _[number]_, whose registered office is at _[address]_ (the "**Company**")

2. **The Assignor:** _[full legal name]_, of _[address]_ (the "**Assignor**")

## Background

A. The Assignor has created, developed, or contributed to certain intellectual property that is relevant to the Company's business.

B. The Company requires full ownership of all such intellectual property to operate its business, protect its assets, and satisfy the requirements of investors and commercial partners.

C. The Assignor agrees to assign all such intellectual property to the Company on the terms set out in this agreement.

## Agreed Terms

### 1. Definitions

**"Intellectual Property"** means all patents, rights to inventions, copyright and related rights, trade marks, business names, domain names, rights in get-up, goodwill, rights in designs, rights in computer software, database rights, rights to use and protect confidential information (including know-how and trade secrets), and all other intellectual property rights, in each case whether registered or unregistered, and including all applications and rights to apply for and be granted renewals or extensions of, and rights to claim priority from, such rights, and all similar or equivalent rights or forms of protection which subsist or will subsist now or in the future in any part of the world.

**"Assigned IP"** means all Intellectual Property:

(a) created, developed, or contributed to by the Assignor at any time prior to the date of this agreement that relates to the business of the Company, including but not limited to the items listed in Schedule 1; and

(b) all Intellectual Property created, developed, or contributed to by the Assignor while engaged by the Company, whether as employee, director, consultant, or contractor.

### 2. Assignment

2.1 The Assignor hereby assigns to the Company, with full title guarantee, all right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP, including all rights of action and remedies in respect of past infringements.

2.2 The assignment in clause 2.1 is effective worldwide and for the full duration of each right assigned, including any renewals or extensions.

2.3 The Assignor irrevocably and unconditionally waives all moral rights under the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 (and any broadly equivalent rights in other jurisdictions) that the Assignor has or may have in any of the Assigned IP.

### 3. Further Assurance

3.1 The Assignor shall, at the Company's request and expense, execute all documents and do all things necessary to give effect to this agreement, including but not limited to:

(a) applying for or supporting the registration of any Intellectual Property rights;

(b) executing formal assignments or other documents required by any intellectual property registry; and

(c) providing information and assistance in connection with any proceedings relating to the Assigned IP.

### 4. Warranties

The Assignor warrants that:

4.1 The Assignor is the sole legal and beneficial owner of the Assigned IP and has full right and authority to assign it.

4.2 The Assigned IP is free from all encumbrances, licences, and third-party rights.

4.3 To the best of the Assignor's knowledge, the Assigned IP does not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.

4.4 The Assignor has not granted any rights or licences in respect of the Assigned IP to any third party.

### 5. Consideration

The Assignor acknowledges that the consideration for the assignments in this agreement includes:

(a) _[£1.00 (one pound sterling)]_ paid by the Company to the Assignor (receipt of which the Assignor acknowledges); and/or

(b) _[the allotment of shares in the Company to the Assignor / the Assignor's continued engagement by the Company / other consideration — specify as appropriate]_.

### 6. Confidentiality

The Assignor shall keep confidential all information relating to the Assigned IP and the Company's business and shall not disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of the Company.

### 7. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

7.1 This agreement is governed by and shall be construed in accordance with the law of England and Wales.

7.2 The courts of England and Wales shall have exclusive jurisdiction to settle any dispute arising out of or in connection with this agreement.
